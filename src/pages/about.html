Clean Energy

We are a passionate team dedicated to making global energy data accessible, understandable, and impactful. Our mission is to empower researchers, policymakers, and the public with reliable insights into renewable energy, energy access, and carbon emissions. By visualizing data from over 200 countries across two decades, we aim to drive awareness, inform decisions, and inspire actions toward a more sustainable and inclusive energy future.

OUR SOLUTION

We offer an interactive platform that visualizes 20 years of renewable energy data from over 200 countries.

Users can explore trends by country and compare two countries side by side, making global energy insights easy to understand and use for sustainable decisions.

The team

This project is a collaboration between undergraduate students from University of Applied Sciences Technikum Wien and King Mongkut's University of Technology North Bangkok. Together, they develop a Java-based platform that visualizes 20 years of global renewable energy data. The team combines skills from both universities to create an interactive tool for exploring and comparing sustainable energy trends worldwide.
