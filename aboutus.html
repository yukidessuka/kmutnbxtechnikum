About Us

We are a passionate team dedicated to making global energy data accessible, understandable, and impactful. Our mission is to empower researchers, policymakers, and the public with reliable insights into renewable energy, energy access, and carbon emissions. By visualizing data from over 200 countries across two decades, we aim to drive awareness, inform decisions, and inspire actions toward a more sustainable and inclusive energy future.
